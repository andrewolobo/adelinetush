About

I am a Software Developer and Educator. I have taught Game Design and Development since 2015. I am currently employed as a Software Developer at BLeafIT in Kampala, Uganda. My areas of expertise include UI/UX, animation, VR Development, Unity 3D, C#, HTML5, and JavaScript.

I have taught Video Game Design at the 2016 and 2017 Technology Camp sponsored by Oysters & Pearls – Uganda at Gulu High School during the January holiday break. I have also taught at Outbox Hub and served as a mentor, teaching and encouraging young women with an interest in technology and Software Programming at the 2015 Technovation Challenge held in Kampala. I received a Bachelor’s Degree with a concentration in Business Administration and Management from Makerere University Kampala in January, 2014. I am passionate about Web App Development and Game Development.

Among my honors, I was the Winner for the “Best mHealth Solution” in 2015, a competition sponsored by MTN Uganda where my teammates and I designed a 3D mobile game, “Run For Your Life,” to create awareness about HIV, other chronic diseases, and general health care. I was also commended as 2nd Runner Up in #HackForYouth, sponsored by UNFPA, whereby at a three-day hackathon, I developed a mobile app solution to promote young people’s access to sexual and reproductive health.

Games

Ten Twenty Four

Ten Twenty Four is an Adventure Survival game. The story revolves around a boy whose village has been destroyed by alien beings. Ther goal of the player is to find Asclepius to save the village and get rid of the aliens and restore health

It's available to download for free on Android Store

Noga

Gigi, the main character in Noga, often visits his village to help his granny at the farm. He is given time durations to finish his chores on the farm. The Noga Game is aimed to informatively create awareness of the wealth of our society in the agricultural and nutritional aspects. The goal of the player is to pick a given number of fruits before time runs out.

Projects

The Helios Project was started in September 2016.

The goal of this project is to teach Game Development using Unity3D online.

It will focus 2D, 3D and VR. All resources used to create tutorials will be available for download for free.

The tutorials, for the start, wiill range from Beginner to Intermediate Level.

Project Goals

Although the focus of the Helios Project is 2D, 3D and VR, we hope to have turorials ready for 2D and 3D in early 2017. We hope to have the first VR tutorial before the end of 2017

In addition to Unity, we hope to teach more about how to make 2D Game Art and 3D models in later tutorials.
